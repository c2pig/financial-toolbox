# Financial Toolbox

Personal financial research ground and analytical workspace.

## Structure

### research/
Active market studies and macro analysis. Time-stamped research notes tracking market dynamics, policy shifts, and structural changes in financial markets.

### barometers/
Systematic tracking of market climate indicators. Monitoring frameworks for key decision-making bodies and market sentiment gauges.

### indicators/
Custom metrics and analytical signals. Quantitative tools for pattern recognition and market condition assessment.

### tools/
Utilities and scripts supporting financial analysis. Computational aids for data processing, modeling, and decision support.

---

A workspace for building financial intuition through structured observation and analysis.
